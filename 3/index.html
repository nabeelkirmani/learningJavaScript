Frequently Asked Questions

What steps should I follow if I need to return an item from my recent order, and how long will it take?
Return Instructions Make sure the item is eligible for return within the return window (usually 30 days from delivery).

Typically, it takes 5-7 business days to process your return and issue a refund after we receive the item. You’ll get an email confirmation once it’s processed. If you have questions, contact our customer service team!

What are the shipping options available?
Shipping Options

We offer several shipping options to accommodate your needs, including standard shipping, expedited shipping, and express delivery. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days, while expedited and express options can reduce delivery times to 2-3 business days or even overnight, depending on your location.

How does your company handle customer inquiries regarding the return policy, particularly in cases where items are received damaged?
Return Policy

Our return policy allows customers to return items within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange, provided the items are in their original condition. If an item arrives damaged or not as described, please reach out to our customer service team immediately. We’ll guide you through the return process, and any damaged items can typically be returned at no additional cost.
